Welcome to the Trivia Night Quiz

Thank you for participating. Before starting please review the following guidelines:

  • Question Format: You will be asked a series of multiple-choice questions with four possible answers.
  • Answer Selection: Choose the correct answer by selecting the corresponding letter (A, B, C, or D).
  • Scoring: Points are awarded for correct answers. Incorrect answers do not affect your score.
  • Feedback: You will receive immediate feedback on your answer, indicating whether it is correct or incorrect.
  • Final Score: At the end of the quiz, your total score will be displayed.

Thank you, and best of luck with the quiz.
